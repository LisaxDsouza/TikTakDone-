# ✅ DoneDone - The "Smart" Todo App

Hello! Welcome to **DoneDone**, a productivity-first To-Do app designed to keep your life organized and your passwords secure—all while looking great in both light and dark modes. 🚀

This app isn't just a list; it's a personal assistant that knows what's urgent and keeps your secrets safe. 

---

## ✨ What's Inside?

### 🧠 Smart Features
- **Smart Sorting**: Forget manual organizing! Our algorithm automatically bubbles the most urgent tasks (based on priority and deadline) to the top of your list.
- **Overdue Alerts**: A friendly (but firm!) banner stays at the top of your screen if you have tasks that have passed their deadline. No more "I forgot" excuses! ⏳

### 🔐 The Secure Vault
- **Private Storage**: Hide your sensitive account details and passwords in a separate, secure Vault.
- **Password Magic**: Generate ultra-secure, 16-character passwords with just one tap.
- **Strength Meter**: Instantly see how tough your password is with our real-time feedback bar. 💪

### 🎨 Personal Touches
- **Midnight & Paper Themes**: Whether you're a night owl or a morning bird, toggle between high-contrast dark and clean light modes with one tap. 🌓
- **Urgency Badges**: Tasks due within 24 hours get a "DUE SOON" badge to help you stay ahead of the game.

---

## 🛠️ The Tech Sauce
The engine under the hood:
- **Core**: React Native CLI + TypeScript (Strict)
- **State**: Redux Toolkit (Slices for everything!)
- **Persistence**: `AsyncStorage` (Saved locally on your device)
- **Engine**: Hermes (Optimized for speed)

---

## 🏁 Getting Started
Ready to test? It's as easy as:
1.  **Extract** the project folder.
2.  **Install**: Run `npm install` in your terminal.
3.  **Start Metro**: `npx react-native start`
4.  **Run on Android**: `npx react-native run-android`
